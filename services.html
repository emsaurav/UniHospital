Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions for you and your family

Cardiology

Our cardiology department offers comprehensive care for heart conditions, including diagnostic tests, interventional procedures, and cardiac rehabilitation programs.

Neurology

Our neurology specialists diagnose and treat disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic department provides treatment for musculoskeletal conditions, including joint replacements, sports injuries, and spine disorders.

Pediatrics

Our pediatric specialists provide comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents, focusing on their unique developmental needs.

Surgery

Our surgical department offers a wide range of procedures, from minimally invasive surgeries to complex operations, using the latest techniques and technologies.

General Medicine

Our general medicine department provides primary healthcare services, including preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment of common illnesses and chronic conditions.

Specialized Services

Advanced care for complex medical conditions

Advanced Diagnostics

Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic technologies, including MRI, CT scans, PET scans, and advanced laboratory services. These tools enable our medical team to accurately diagnose conditions and develop effective treatment plans.

We also offer specialized diagnostic services such as cardiac catheterization, endoscopy, and neurophysiological testing to provide comprehensive evaluations for complex medical conditions.

Preventive Care

Keeping you healthy is our priority

Wellness Programs

At MediCare Hospital, we believe that prevention is better than cure. Our comprehensive wellness programs are designed to help you maintain optimal health and prevent illness.

Our preventive care services include regular health check-ups, immunizations, health risk assessments, lifestyle counseling, and screening tests for early detection of diseases.

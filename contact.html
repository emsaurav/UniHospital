How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointment line at +91898989898, using our online appointment form, or visiting our hospital in person. We recommend scheduling appointments in advance to ensure availability.

What insurance plans do you accept?

How can I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through our patient portal, which is available 24/7. You can also request physical copies of your records by submitting a written request to our medical records department.

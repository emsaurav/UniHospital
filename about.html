Our History

Founded in 2005, UNI चिकित्सालय has grown from a small clinic to a comprehensive healthcare facility serving thousands of patients each year. Our journey has been marked by a steadfast commitment to excellence and innovation in healthcare delivery.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities, enhanced our technology, and recruited top medical professionals to ensure that we provide the highest quality of care to our patients.
